· CLEVER PUZZLES FOR CURIOUS MINDS ·

World-renowned detective Loup is excited when his children Sherry and Bo express interest in following his footsteps. To teach them, he assembles a series of toy models based on the strangest crimes in history...

Little Locked Rooms is a mystery game where you solve crimes seemingly impossible to perform. Inspect the crime scenes in the shape of tiny dioramas and figure out the tricks used by the criminal masterminds!
